Rules

  1. Character must remain a freelancer until at least one of the jobs is available.
  2. Character may switch freely between the assigned jobs as the main job until one job is mastered.
  3. Once a job is mastered, it cannot be the main job until all jobs are mastered.
    • If one job is mastered but all other jobs are unavailable, chracter must remain as the mastered job until another job is available.
  4. Secondary job may be set to any jobs the character is assigned at any time.
  5. Once all jobs are mastered, character may switch freely between jobs.
  6. This is just for fun. Feel free to alter the rules to make it more fun.
